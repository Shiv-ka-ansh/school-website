Admission Process

Our straightforward admission process ensures a smooth transition for your child into our school community.

1

Inquiry & Information

Contact our admissions office or submit an online inquiry to learn about available seats and requirements.

2

Application Form

Complete the admission application form with all required details and attach necessary documents.

3

Interaction & Assessment

Student and parent interaction with our faculty to understand learning needs and school expectations.

4

Admission Confirmation

Upon selection, complete the admission formalities and fee payment to confirm your child's seat.
