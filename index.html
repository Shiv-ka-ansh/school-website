About SMPS Jhansi

Established in 1998, Siddheshwar Mountain Public School has been a beacon of quality education in Jhansi, nurturing young minds with values, knowledge, and skills for the future.

Our Mission

To provide holistic education that develops intellectual, emotional, physical, and spiritual potential of every student, preparing them to be responsible global citizens.

Our Vision

To be recognized as a leading educational institution that nurtures innovation, creativity, and excellence while maintaining strong moral and ethical values.
